More about me!

I'm Amir Mrabet, a dedicated and ambitious first-year software engineering student at the North American School in Sfax, Tunisia. I am 26 years old and was born and raised in Sfax, Tunisia.

My academic journey has been great, with a strong focus on both technical and theoretical aspects of computer science. I have obtained 3 diplomas since 2015/2016 when I passed my baccalaureate.

I studied management computing at FSEG in Sfax, where I received my bachelor's degree last year with distinction. Additionally, I have two diplomas in the agricultural field, which demonstrate my diverse interests and ability to excel in multiple fields.

Regarding my hobbies, I am an enthusiastic selenophile, which means I am deeply passionate about the moon. I am also an avid amateur astronomer, and I enjoy observing and studying the night sky. I often take photos of the moon and other celestial objects through my telescope and camera, which is my way to express my love for the beauty of the universe. My passion for astrophotography began in 2015 when I received a DSLR camera as a gift, and since then the astronomy world has been my escape and source of inspiration.

I am also a passionate home cook and baker, who enjoys experimenting with different recipes and ingredients in the kitchen. I have a keen interest in food photography, and I enjoy capturing the beauty of my culinary creations through the lens of my camera. Furthermore, I am a nature lover, who enjoys spending time in the great outdoors, hiking and observing the beauty of nature.
